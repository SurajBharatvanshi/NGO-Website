About EcoBridge

EcoBridge is an NGO focused on sustainable development and community transformation. Since 2016, we have impacted 10,000+ lives by providing clean water, quality education, ecological restoration, and supporting women’s empowerment.
Achievements:

  • Planted 50,000+ trees in degraded areas
  • Built rainwater harvesting for 15 villages
  • Provided skill workshops for women and youth
  • Conducted 120+ health and hygiene camps

Our Projects

Supporting People & Nature, Year-Round

Reforestation Drive

Restoring green cover by planting local species with the help of villagers and schoolchildren.

Clean Water Access

Building wells and teaching families water sanitation best practices for healthy living.

Women Empowerment

Vocational training and microloans for women entrepreneurs to build resilient communities.

Healthcare Camps

Organizing free medical check-ups, distributing medicines, and raising awareness about preventive healthcare.

Education for All

Providing school supplies, scholarships, and after-school programs to ensure every child has access to learning.

Wildlife Protection

Safeguarding endangered species through awareness campaigns and habitat conservation efforts.

Disaster Relief

Providing emergency food, shelter, and medical aid to families affected by floods, droughts, and other natural disasters.

Youth Skill Development

Training young people in digital literacy, vocational skills, and entrepreneurship to prepare them for future opportunities.

Renewable Energy Initiatives

Installing solar panels and promoting clean energy solutions to reduce carbon footprints in rural communities.

Food Security Program

Supporting farmers with sustainable practices and distributing nutritious meals to underprivileged families.

Animal Welfare

Rescuing stray animals, providing veterinary care, and running adoption drives to ensure safe homes for them.

Community Gardens

Creating shared green spaces where families can grow vegetables, learn sustainable farming, and strengthen community bonds.
